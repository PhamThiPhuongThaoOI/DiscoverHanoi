Welcome to Hanoi

Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam, is one of the leading cities in the country in terms of development. It is not only known for its well-preserved classical architecture from wartime, such as the Opera House, the Old Quarter, the One Pillar Pagoda, and more, but also vibrant with modern technological advancements, especially in entertainment technologies like Game-boxes and VR.
This blend of classical and modern elements creates a unique atmosphere and quality of life in Hanoi, offering distinct experiences to its inhabitants and visitors alike. When it comes to Hanoi, one can truly immerse themselves in the city's essence through enjoyable outings and experiences with friends and family.
The suggestions provided in this article aim to assist you in making the best choices for yourself and your family, ensuring a memorable time in Hanoi.

Featured Places

  1. BoxDance

    The first place we cannot miss in the list of new discoveries in Hanoi today is BoxGame. Inspired by a fitness game from the United States, Box Dance Fitness Gaming has made its debut in Hanoi, creating an explosive effect among the youth. This event has garnered a lot of attention, with videos of participants becoming top trending with millions of views on TikTok, attracting young people across the country.
    Box Dance Fitness Gamingis not only an entertainment game but also an effective form of exercise. This system aims at two major goals: enhancing endurance and flexibility to improve physical health, and stimulating quick reflexes by gradually increasing speed and difficulty levels. With this appeal, Box Dance has quickly become a favorite destination, with fully booked sessions on weekends.
    So, what are you waiting for? Let’s get there quickly!

  2. Jum Arena FlagShip

    The second exciting destination that we cannot miss is JUMP ARENA.
    With over 20 physical games, JUMP ARENA is not just a trampoline park but a paradise of dynamism. It's a place where everyone can freely express their personality, explore themselves, grow together, and become more connected.
    We can have fun with friends or teammates while increasing our body’s flexibility and enhancing our personal agility. Moreover, this activity gives us time to challenge ourselves with physical difficulties that we have never experienced anywhere else. Hopefully, this will be a place where active young people can become more energetic and reduce stress after working hard. Book your tickets here now!

  3. Central Circus

    If you're too familiar with movies shown on the big screen and feel they lack real-life interaction, and you’re looking for something new and exciting, this third destination can help you rediscover a part of your childhood and offer you even more fun.
    Located in the heart of Hanoi, the Central Circus is a must-visit for anyone who loves circus arts. With thrilling and creative performances, from aerial acrobatics to hilarious clown acts, the Central Circus provides wonderful entertainment for audiences of all ages. It’s not just a place to enjoy top-notch circus acts, but also a space to connect with family and friends through colorful and emotional experiences. Come and explore the magical world of circus arts at the Central Circus!

  4. Vietnam Museum of Ethnology

    Returning to a space imbued with historical beauty, the fourth destination, the Museum of Ethnology, is not just a place for deep understanding of the past, but also an environment to experience and feel the reality of historical artifacts. Located in the heart of Hanoi, the Museum of Ethnology takes us on a captivating journey to explore the culture and history of the Vietnamese people.
    With thousands of precious artifacts, this museum truly is a treasure trove of the life and traditions of the 54 ethnic groups across the country. Here, you can admire traditional costumes, labor tools, and exquisite folk art pieces. Beyond preserving cultural values, the Museum of Ethnology offers enriching educational experiences, helping you gain a deeper understanding of the ethnic heritage.
    Come and discover the richness and diversity of Vietnamese culture at the Museum of Ethnology, where the past and present blend seamlessly in each artifact and story. This is truly an experience not to be missed there!

  5. The West Lake Flower Valley

    And the final destination will truly be a memorable experience for those who love photography and adore flowers with exciting outdoor activities: the West Lake Flower Valley.
    The West Lake Flower Valley is likened to the heart of Tay Ho District, Hanoi, and is a natural wonder amidst the bustling urban area. Located only 600 meters from West Lake Water Park, this area spans 7,000 square meters, showcasing a vibrant tapestry with hundreds of unique flower species, from radiant sunflowers to gentle lavender.
    Gathering a variety of exquisite and diverse flowers such as daisies, chrysanthemums, irises, marigolds, and sunflowers. Particularly, butterfly orchids, lilies, and marigolds are the standout flowers here. When visiting, you will surely be captivated by the shimmering beauty and colors of the West Lake Flower Valley.
    Furthermore, there are dining and entertainment activities, as well as ample outdoor space for relaxation and bonding. Especially during the evening, you have the opportunity to listen to music and engage in heartfelt conversations.
    It's truly an exciting place, isn't it? Come and experience the exploration together!!!
